About Us

Isiqongo Engineering

Isiqongo Engineering is a 100% black-owned South African company based in Secunda, Mpumalanga. We deliver reliable Civil, Mechanical, and Electrical Engineering solutions to the mining and industrial sectors, driven by technical expertise and operational excellence.

Our vision is to become a recognized leader in engineering solutions by consistently delivering exceptional results. We execute every project with efficiency, safety, and quality — ensuring it is done right the first time.
